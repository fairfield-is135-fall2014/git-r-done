Game Plan

While the main event on the Business Plan Competition calendar is the Finale in April, the competition actually starts long before that. It pays to have a strategy that takes into account all of the resources and opportunties provided to you as a competitor in the six months between the Kickoff in September and the Finale in April.

In order to get the most out of your experience, and to heighten your chances of winning the competition, the following 6-step progression is strongly recommended:

  1. Do your homework: know the rules, check important dates and deadlines, consider the market and competition, etc.
  2. Network like crazy, gaining access to people and resources you will need later.
  3. Form your team carefully: it takes the right partners to build and sustain a business.
  4. Connect with an experienced mentor. They've been there. They can help!
  5. Consult with experts who can do things you can't.
  6. Close the deal with the best team, best plan, and best pitch possible.
